Grandma's Lemon Mergingue Pie

One-9-inch pie

30 min- Prep time

10 Min - Cook Time

40 Min - Total

8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all- purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons. juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1(9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven:

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F°(175 degrees C°)

  2. Make Lemon Filling:

    3. In a medium saucepan ...

    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch , and salt.
    • strin in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Stir in butter
    • Place egg yolks in small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into baked pastrey shell.
  3. Make Mergingue:

    4. In a large glass or metal bowl ...

    • Whip egg whites until foamy
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread Meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake:

    5. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
